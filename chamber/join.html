Membership options

Bronze membership

  • Monthly Fee: $4.99
  • Benefits:
    • Access to basic features and services.
    • Limited support.
    • Occasional discounts on select products and services
  • Ideal for individuals or small organizations with minimal needs

Silver membership

  • Monthly Fee: $9.99
  • Benefits:
    • Access to premium features and services.
    • Priority support with faster response times.
    • Exclusive content or resources.
    • 10% discount on all products or services.
  • Suitable for small businesses or individuals seeking additional support and perks.

Gold membership

  • Monthly Fee: $19.99
  • Benefits:
    • All features of the Silver Membership.
    • Enhanced support with dedicated account manager.
    • Access to advanced tools and resources.
    • 15% discount on all products or services.
    • Quarterly webinars or workshops.
  • Ideal for medium-sized businesses or professionals requiring more comprehensive assistance and resources.

NP membership

  • Monthly Fee: $0
  • Benefits:
    • All features of the Gold Membership.
    • Free access to premium services.
    • Dedicated support team for non-profits.
    • Priority consideration for special initiatives or partnerships.
    • Recognition as a valued supporter of the organization's mission.
  • Exclusively available for registered non-profit organizations, providing them with the necessary tools and support to further their charitable endeavors.
